SMF Playback


Standard MIDI Files (SMFs) can be loaded by clicking on the Load SMF button. Clicking on the Play and Stop buttons controls playback. The loaded SMF will be played using the hosted synth. Note that the beat counter is a simple activity indicator in that it approximates the current measure number and beat and assumes 4/4 time for the SMF.
